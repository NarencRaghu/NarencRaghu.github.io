Piano

I have been learning piano for 10 years and counting. Throughout this journey of music, I have found a way to relax and escape the stress of the real world. The music I have learnt ranges from Classical Era music such as Beethoven's Moonlight Sonata to Romantic Era music such as March of the Trolls. I am fascinated by this diversity in music and am excited to continue learning much more about piano. I am currently learning Jazz music alongside Classical and Baroque music, specifically Arabesque by Debussy, and the Tempest Sonata by Bach.

My Achievements

While studying and learning to play the piano, I took part in the Arizona Study Program for 10 years, completing the final level 12A in May 2020. Upon earning the Superior with Distinction award in this level, I earned the James Anthony Senior Scholarship Award for exceptional performance in this program.

Additionally, I took part in several concerts where I displayed my knowledge and perfection in the art of piano. These experiences showed me the joy that music brings to people and I continued to pursue piano in order to spread this joy to as many people as I can.
