My Projects

Alzheimer's Prediction Project

The goal of this project is to predict instances of Alzheimer's disease in patients after they have completed an MRI scan. By predicting who had Alzheimer's, we are able to save people by putting them into a preventative care treatment before they are even diagnosed with the disease.

Personalized Cancer Medicine Project

There are various treatment approaches for Cancer but often times, choosing the most promising approach will be difficult. The goal is to predict the most promising method of addressing cancer for each individual patient, creating a personalized cancer treatment.
